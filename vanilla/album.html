Pipeline Stages Explained

Mirror

Each image is flipped horizontally — a simple canvas pixel operation.

Gaussian Blur

Converts to grayscale then applies a 3×3 weighted average to reduce noise before edge detection.

Non-Max Suppression

Sobel gradients are computed; only local maxima along the gradient direction are kept, thinning edges to 1 px.

Hysteresis

Double threshold classifies pixels as strong / weak / non-edge. Weak edges connected to strong ones survive.
